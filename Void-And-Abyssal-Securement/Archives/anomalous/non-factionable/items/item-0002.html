[NF] Item-0002

Name: Pain

Object Class: Safe

Object Danger: Xernox

Description: It is a small, red pill. When consumed, all pleasure the consumer feels is converted into pain. This conversion happens at a b(400)x^(2+b) of the pleasure caused, in which b is the amount of pills consumed. This means, that it scales exponentially to the amount of pills consumed. Along with inflicting pain, the amount of power the user receives is the same amount multiplication of pain, multiplied with their remaining energy. It is a massive increase of power, however it will kill the user quite fast unless used with Item-0001.

Upon consumption of both Item-0001 and Item-0002, the pleasure and pain caused by both are cancelled out. However the energy multiplicity from pain and pleasure creation are still accounted for, causing an infinite increase in energy at the speed the cells can die. The cells are then revived instantaneously causing a never ending increase in power, until the pills effects wear off, in approximately 4 minutes.

When either pill are taken at less then their full amount, the user feels a large increase in lethargy, and undergo cyclicodelic affects, however with a strong high instead of weak. They will not feel pain or pleasure, but their bodily will get continuously weaker due to the draining of nutrients. This nutrience drain will continue until the consumer either wakes up from the drug, or until they are deceased.
