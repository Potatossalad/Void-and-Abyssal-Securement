The Nexus

Detail(s): "Time itself is a fickle subject that ebbs and flows in unknown ways."

Description: Our universe has been severed from the [ARROW OF TIME]. A temporary solution was created, The Nexus. However, it has become more of a permanent solution, as there has been no observable way to return our direct connection to the Arrow.

To begin, we must say that even if a universe is separated from the Arrow, objects with the universe are not. They must be tied manually, however. The Nexus is bound with every soul in the universe, as it requires energy to function and the ambient hum of souls supplies enough power. It should be noted that every middle being now has 3 souls (higher and lower beings have 2 souls). A Planar Soul, the Abyssal Twin and a Nexus Soul. The Planar Soul (by extension, the Abyssal Twin) and the Nexus Soul are entangled, meaning if one is dissipated, the other is dissipated as well. This is not a problem, as nothing can interact with the Nexus Souls, so the life of a soul is only bound by its Planar Soul (or Abyssal Twin, which ever is strong).

The Nexus acts as an artificial bridge between the universe and the Arrow. Even though the universe is separated, it can still be tied to another object. In this case, the Universe is tied to the Nexus. The Arrow can also be tied to an object, though the process is much more metaphysical than physical compared to tying an object to the Universe. As such, the Nexus was designed for the usage of Souls, the most metaphysical thing we are aware of. This completes the metaphysical requirement between bridging the Arrow with an object, therefore completing the ritual of reconnecting the universe to the time-stream, protecting our universe from becoming a [TIMELOST] universe.
