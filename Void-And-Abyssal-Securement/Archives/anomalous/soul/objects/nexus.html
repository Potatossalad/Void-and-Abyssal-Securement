The Nexus

Detail(s): "Time itself is a fickle subject that ebbs and flows in unknown ways."

Description: Our universe has been severed from the [ARROW OF TIME]. A temporary solution was created, The Nexus. However, it has become more of a permanent solution, as there has been no observable way to return our direct connection to the Arrow.

To begin, we must say that even if a universe is separated from the Arrow, objects with the universe are not. They must be tied manually, however. The Nexus is bound with every soul in the universe, as it requires energy to function and the ambient hum of souls supplies enough power. It should be noted that every middle being now has 3 souls (higher and lower beings have 2 souls). An Abyssal Soul, A Planar Soul, and a Nexus Soul. The Planar Soul and the Nexus Soul are entangled, meaning if one is dissipated, the other is dissipated as well. This is not a problem, as nothing can interact with the Nexus Souls, so the life of a soul is only bound by its Planar Soul.
