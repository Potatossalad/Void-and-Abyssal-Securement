Void Stone

Forewarning

This is a mock-up document submitted by Researcher 2408-IIs of V.A.A.S as this material is still very unknown.

Physical Description

  • Rough, yet somehow smooth (how?)
  • Brittle (until compressed)
  • Hot (until compressed)
  • Black in color
  • When disturbed it glows faintly along a gradient
  • Internal Crystalline Structure (until compressed)
  • Dense. Immensely dense.
  • Infinitely compressible. I.E. you can just keep adding to it

Properties When Compressed

  • Smooth, but sticky (why?)
  • Incredibly durable (beats stellar steel)
  • It exponentially gets colder the more it gets compressed (how can we exploit this?)
  • The more it is compressed, the lighter it gets (Inverse Relationship)
  • Its borders become a solid color of either neon cyan, neon pink or neon purple (Why these colors in particular?). The placements of the border is made by the eye, not the stone itself.

Location

  • Only in Pure Void (No Shit) as asteroids
  • Only seen in the rim of the void. None of been seen as as we get closer to the center of Pure Void.
    • The outer rims are the hottest part of the void. Does this have anything to do with their placement?

Theory

Void Stone may have been the item that contained all matter that made up the universe prior to the Big Bang. This is theorized due to its property of being able to be compressed infinitely without A) Turning into a black hole and B) it can be decompressed (unlike a black hole).

The event that caused the rapid decompression of the pre-universe may have been ether-field fluctuations resulting in enough space for Zero-Point Energy (ZPE) to have been added to the system. Due to the large density of the system, even the miniscule amount of space that was created could make an amount of ZPE equal to the amount of energy in the universe³.
