The Volturnis

Name: Volturnis

Description: They are the Precursor Race of Humans. Very little information is known about them, however their discovered DNA matches 99% to that of the human genome, with very slight DNA changes. These changes most likely cause an increase in their intelligence, and their lack of aging.

Their existence was unearthed from a structure found within the core of Terra (Earth, for those in another timeline). This structure houses the Theorem of Earth, the Theorem of Life, and the Continuous Theorem. It is how we learned that they had technology or divinity that could alter fate.

Technology:

  1. They had Interstellar Travel Systems. Whilst not ships like we are used to, they had large gateways, fueled by quantum properties. It appears they had a form of technology for large gaps in space along with these gates, as their homeworld was found 4.543 billion light years away, the age of Earth (as of this time).
  2. They had technology that could alter Fate itself (see above). The properties the theorems are mostly unknown, but the known information will be simplified.

    The Theorem of Earth states that Terra/Earth, upon complete destruction, will be completely remade, resembling the last stable period of life.

    The Theorem of Life states that whenever life on Earth/Terra is destroyed, it will activate The Theorem of Earth, as well as boosting the time speed of the planet until intelligent life redevelops. It also activates upon completion of the Theorem of Earth.

    The Continuous Theorem essentially dictates that where ever pieces of the sanctuary (the structure in the core), will also undergo the Theorem of Earth. Pieces of the sanctuary are on in Luna, as well as the Sun. The Volturnis species did not exist in the Supra universe.

    This activation is also differed. If Luna or Sol were to be destroyed, only that body will be reformed (Though if Sol dies, Terra also dies because of the nova). Upon the destruction of Terra, both Sol and Luna will also reform, but won't reform in a Theorem of Life scenario.
  3. They possessed extended knowledge on quantum properties. Within the Sanctuary were 2 gates (explained above). The gates were octogonal in design. These gates were supplied ith a "Quantum Liquid".When the gates were powered (through usage of cables, as power could not betransmitted wirelessly into it), the gates activated. The area inside turned into a purple array. Anything that paassed through one gate, would appear throught the opposite gate (like a Portal Gun).

Footnotes:

Precursor Race: A Precursor Race is a species, alien or not, that formed another species. This term is given one of two ays. The first way, is when the Precursor engineered the new race, usually genetically. The second way is if the new species is the last remnant of the precursor, the possibility of the races meeting each other is rare, if not impossible.

Quantum Liquid: Composed of the possible instances of a liquid. The liquid itself is unknown, however it can be instantiated into any other liquid, the energy requirements getting higher depending on the amount of bonds and elements that the liquid has. It is frequently instantiated into water and liquid naquadah within the UE (the pool in the Sanctuary is seemingly neverending).
