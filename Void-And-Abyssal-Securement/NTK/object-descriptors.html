Object Descriptors

Object Classes

The chance of escaping. Uncontained entitie (such as other species), do not have a class.

Safe: Easy to recontain.

Sotna: Object has a somewhat decent chance of escaping (desire to escape not taken into account).

Preti: Object has a high chance of escaping.

Cleto: Object assists in recontainment.

Theta: Guarenteed to either escape or occur. Effectively, an incoming apocalypse.

Object Danger Level

How dangerous the object is. Every cataloged object (other than history/stories) has a danger level.

Safe: Not dangerous in any way (unless brandished as a weapon (duh)).

Reta: Small danger level. Unable to kill, and causes mild harm, however it wouldn't break a bone.

Itera: Somewhat dangerous. Possibility to kill, althought unlikely to. Can causes serious harm.

Verno: Dangerous. Very likely to kill.

Xernox: No chance of surviving an encounter, unless heavily monitored or supplied other objects.

Object Abilites

Known dangers.

None: No known abilities.

Psionic: Has psionic abilities.

Cognitohazard: Being percieved is a hazard.

Ontological: Being linked to conscious ideas can cause danger.

Physical: Immensly physically adept.

Unstoppable: Does not refer to invulnerable or immortal. Simply means that the object will stop at nothing to get what it wants achieved (I.E, enraged SCP-096).
