Void And Abyssal Securement

Who Are We?

We are a collective Mult-Ultraversal group built on containing Void and Abyssal Entities. Due to the large presence we have within the Godsverses, we have also become curators of humanity's knowledge. We remain unknown to the worlds government until they achieve interstellar travel. It's been observed that a civilization it is unlikely for a united people to not exist once achieving interstellar travel.

Why Not Soul Entities?

The Soul Pressure exerted by soul entities make it so we can not even attempt to get close to them, preventing us from containing them. They also have no abilities that can not be replicated with Resonance.

Why Do We Exist?

Many worlds have been destroyed due to Void and Abyssal entities, and there is much to be learned from their abilities. The ability to travel through the void, to teleport, to ascend planes. These abilities have all been unlocked and achieved by humanity through our efforts.
