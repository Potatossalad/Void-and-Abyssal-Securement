Intelligence Scales

Non-sentient = No thinking mind.

Sentient = A thinking mind capable of forming and responding to emotion.

Sapient = A thinking mind, capable of pondering things unnecessary for survival.

Human = Intelligence is that of a human.

Greater than human = Elevated levels of intelligence than that of a human. The range starts from that of Einstein's level of intelligence and extends to ~400IQ. Some humans have achieved this level.

Severe = Capable of performing all human math problems within their head. An estimated starting IQ of 400+. A singular human has achieved this level of intelligence: Pilot-1729.

Severe* = Hyper-intelligence considered unfathomable. IQ of 1500+. The most well-known species to fit this description are the The Skivs.

Important information

As intelligence increases, violent tendencies increase, despite popular belief of the contrary. The reason for this cause is unknown. Popular belief for the contrary is spawned by a majority of the intelligent humans tend to be benevolent, however this is due to neurodivergency, and is not a normal phenomena within the galaxy. This is why corrupt individuals tend to dominate industries. They were intelligence, and the curse of intelligence drives the need to cause misery.
