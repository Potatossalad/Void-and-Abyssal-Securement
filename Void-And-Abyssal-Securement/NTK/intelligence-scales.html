Intelligence Scales

Non-sentient = No thinking mind.

Sentient = A thinking mind capable of forming and responding to emotion.

Sapient = A thinking mind, capable of pondering things unnecessary for survival.

Human = Intelligence is that of a human.

Greater than human = Elevated levels of intelligence than that of a human. The range starts from that of Einstein's level of intelligence and extends to ~400IQ. Some humans have achieved this level.

Severe = Capable of performing all human math problems within their head. An estimated starting IQ of 400+.

Severe* = Hyper-intelligence considered unfathomable. IQ of 1500+. The most well-known species to fit this description are the Skivs.

Important information

As intelligence increases, violent tendencies increase, despite popular belief of the contrary. The reason for this cause is unknown in species that aren't human. Explanation for humans is in the [PILOT-1729] file.

This is a large claim, as in popular belief a majority of the intelligent humans tend to be benevolent, however this is due to neurodivergency, and is not a normal phenomena within the galaxy. This is why corrupt individuals tend to dominate industries. They were intelligence, and the curse of intelligence drives the need to cause misery.

Another factor of intelligence is that there is a biological 'limit'. This 'limit' is not a true limit. It does not mean that intelligence is capped at this level, but rather that past this point, apparent IQ decreases, however processing speed and reaction time increase, making up for this loss in IQ. The human biological cap is exactly 400IQ. A large majority of the human representatives in the galactic counsel have IQ higher than this, though their apparent IQ is closer to 350, due to said biological limit.

Entities can be born with more than their biological limit for IQ. The best example is the Skivs, as their biological limit is 1500IQ, however they have 2500 IQ. This means their apparent IQ is around 1350 IQ. Apparent IQ is difficult to determine, as it is also affected by processing structure (i.e. brain. Most extraterrestrial species lack a central structure for information processing) and their various biochemicals.

Pilot-1729 has an IQ of exactly 400, meaning it has the most peak mental faculties of any human, as shown by its achievements described in its personnel file. It do not have the fastest natural processing time. Though, this is a moot point as every being can have processing speeds optimized in various ways.
